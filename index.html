Get To Know Us.

Limra Solution (PTY) LTD! We are a dynamic and forward-thinking 50% Black Economic Empowerment Company, proudly 50% youth-owned and managed. Established in 2018, our journey began with limited resources but boundless determination. Today, we are rapidly emerging as a key player in the realms of transportation, mining, plant hire, construction, and maintenance services.

At Limra Solution, we believe in "Adding Value to Yours." Our foundation rests on fostering enduring partnerships with our clients, ensuring a deep understanding of their needs. Through dedicated project teams boasting industry expertise, we offer highly competent and proficient services tailored to each endeavor. We integrate capital costs, technical considerations, risk factors, sustainability, and operational dynamics, shaping the long-term success of every project.

Guided by our resolute mission, we uphold the highest work ethics, prioritizing productivity and accountability. Quality is our hallmark, speaking volumes without compromising on safety and reliability.

Each project, regardless of scale, receives our meticulous attention to detail. Our team, led by Mr. Ananias Sekere Sekhaulelo, embodies our core values of customer-centricity, teamwork, transparency, and professionalism. Through our management, operations, and administrative prowess, we assure excellence from start to finish.

What We Do.

Our comprehensive suite of services at Limra Solution (PTY) LTD is poised to transform industries and redefine standards. With a steadfast focus on excellence, we offer expertise across multiple sectors, including mining, transportation, construction, plant hire, and maintenance. Our mining services encompass responsible resource extraction, ensuring sustainable practices for a brighter future. In the realm of transportation, we provide efficient and reliable logistics solutions that keep industries moving forward. When it comes to construction and maintenance, our commitment to quality shines through in every project, building the foundations of progress and ensuring operational efficiency. Our plant hire services offer top-tier machinery, tailored to projects of all scales. At Limra Solution, our services are more than just offerings : they represent our dedication to adding value to your operations, fostering partnerships, and crafting solutions that stand the test of time.
